80's Hairstyles

Mullet The traditional mullet is defined by long hair all over with a longer style in the back. The modern mullet is a short haircut with taper faded sides, short hair in the front, and longer hair in the back. This hairstyle has actually recently made a comeback in the 2000's.
Perm The perm look in the '80s was all about the extra volume, curly bangs, tight coils, and lots and lots and lots of hairspray. Hair was meant to reach record-breaking heights back then, so frizzy texture with lots of back combing was the look du jour.
